Objective

The goal of the Snake Game is to control the snake, guiding it to eat food and grow longer without colliding with its own body or the game borders.

Controls

Use the arrow keys (up, down, left, right) to control the snake's direction. Be strategic in your movements to avoid collisions and maximize your score.

Game Over

The game ends if the snake collides with its own body or the game borders. The score is based on the number of food items eaten and the length of the snake.
